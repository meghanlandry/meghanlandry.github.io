Don't break the silence



I drew a set of cute animals in my favorite color palette for our prompt, "Don't break the silence." The image is drawn in Photoshop. It's intended to be reminiscent of the Hear No Evil, See No Evil, Speak No Evil monkey decorations. Their paws are covering their mouths so that they don't "break the silence."


These are the other two images created by the other two members of my group, Evan Villaflora and Jason Lin respectively.
