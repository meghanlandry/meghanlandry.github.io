ART 103 - Reading 2

1. Banksy’s art is more political and is done without permission. Walter De Maria is more of a conceptual artist who is exploring ideas that aren’t so political, and his execution of his work seems more traditional.

2. All of the sign art kind of subverts expectations. They’re things that are the opposite of what would usually be on the particular sign. They kind of make a joke.

3. In Chicago there are a bunch of public art things. I remember the bean especially, which is really titled Cloud Gate. The artist works a lot with mirrors, and this work is a giant bean shaped mirror that reflects and warps everything around it.
