self as source
SPYGLASS

An expression of the importance of a companion animal (or lack thereof) in coping with anxiety...

SPYGLASS | digital photography



As someone who has some difficulty maintaining friendships, a cat has always been a really important source of comfort to deal with stress and anxiety. When I moved to California, my living situation no longer allowed me to have a cat, so I had to leave my friend with my mom. Sometimes I get really, really sad about this, and on one of those occasions my boyfriend got me a little stuffed cat that looks like my cat. For this project, I photographed myself looking at the plush cat through my class notebook, which I think communicates distance.

INKTOBER

a documentation of hobby art through the persepctive of a creator...

INKTOBER | digital photography











This photo series intends to document the first few days of "Inktober" (which is an informal art event in which artists create a daily ink-based drawing, usually according to a theme). I wanted to record these photos not as a professional artist making art for a gallery, but as a hobby artist creating art for leisure.

attraction / repulsion
MR. SOGGY SOCKS

work intended to create discomfort in the viewer through an attractive display of repulsive subject matter

MR. SOGGY SOCKS | digital photography





















This photo series is of socks in a wet environment. It shows the discomfort of wearing wet socks and of worn, dirty socks. It attempts to frame something uncomfortable and mundane as something beautiful.

A SELF PORTRAIT IN TEN PARTS FOR TEN AUDIENCES

work exploring the idea of the multiple selves we present to different people and the threads that unify them

A SELF PORTRAIT IN TEN PARTS FOR TEN AUDIENCES | digital photography





















This final project returns to and reimagines Self as Source as a topic. It portrays some of the many selves of the work's creator that are adapted and presented for different interactions and relationships.
