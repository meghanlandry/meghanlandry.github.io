Hello!

As you might have noticed, my name is Meghan Landry, and I'm a Digital Media Art student at San Jose State University. I've always been interested in drawing and painting, both digitally and traditionally, and I've worked really hard to develop those skills into something I can hope to get a job with. While I'm getting my Bachelor's and starting my career a little later than I would have liked, I hope it turns out to be better late than never. Someday, I'd love to be a concept artist or create assets and illustrations for games or other media.

I earned my Associate's at Spoon River College in Canton, Illinois and moved to San Jose in 2016 to be with my boyfriend. After some difficulty finding a stable job relevant to my aspirations, I've chosen to return to school to get my Bachelor's in the hopes that I can learn a broader range of skills and have a degree to back them up.

I like creating designs and characters and showing a story through illustrations of them. I like to explore ways to manipulate popular tropes and archetypes to create something that's both familiar and novel to the viewer. I don't enjoy creating art that is critical of society or that makes heavy-handed statements, and I won't unless required to by a class. It feels like this possibly invalidates me as an artist, but I don't want to be something I'm not!

In any case, I hope you enjoy this portfolio!
