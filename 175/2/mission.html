At MASCANNON we aim to put others’ responsibility into your hands. Like everyone else, we want all of this pandemic nonsense to be over, but unlike a significant portion of the US population, we recognize that ignoring the problem or pretending it doesn’t exist does not make it disappear. In all likelihood, herd immunity or a vaccine are hundreds of thousands more deaths away. We offer an elegant, satisfying stopgap to mitigate the spread of COVID-19 from individuals who refuse to take responsibility for themselves.
