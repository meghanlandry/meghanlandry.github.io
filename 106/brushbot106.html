Brush Bot Bunny






I would've liked this to be a little tidier, but I had snapped the leg off of my LED, disintegrated a significant portion of the motor wire, and twisted the resistor into a pretty sorry state, so I just left it in hopes of not damaging anything any further.

I made it into a bunny using some pieces of wire and the cotton from a bottle of vitamins. I thought this would be cute and maybe distract from the mess on top. It doesn't really get very far, but it's very good at spinning.
